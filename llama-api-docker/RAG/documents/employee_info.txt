# AutoConfig Employee Guide

## Company Overview
AutoConfig is a leading AI solutions provider specializing in deploying and managing AI systems for enterprise clients. Our mission is to empower businesses with cutting-edge AI technologies while maintaining the highest standards of security, ethics, and performance.

## Employee Benefits
- Comprehensive healthcare package including dental and vision
- 401(k) matching up to 5% of salary
- 20 days paid time off annually, plus 10 federal holidays
- Flexible work arrangements including remote work options
- Professional development allowance of $2,000 per year
- Gym membership reimbursement
- Mental health services through our EAP program

## Company Policies
### Work Hours
Standard work hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM local time, Monday through Friday. Flexible scheduling is available with manager approval.

### Remote Work
Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days per week, with specific arrangements to be approved by their direct manager.

### Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted semi-annually in June and December. These reviews assess goal achievement, skill development, and alignment with company values.

### Data Security
All employees must adhere to our data security protocols:
- Use company-provided VPN when accessing internal systems remotely
- Enable 2FA for all company accounts
- Never share login credentials
- Encrypt sensitive customer data
- Report security incidents immediately to security@autoconfig.com

## AI Systems at AutoConfig
Our company develops and maintains several AI systems:

### LLM Deployment Framework
Our flagship product allows enterprises to deploy large language models on their own infrastructure with enhanced security and customization options.

### RAGSystem
Our Retrieval-Augmented Generation system provides context-aware responses by retrieving information from company knowledge bases before generating responses.

### ComplianceGuard
An AI system that ensures all AI-generated content complies with regulatory requirements and company policies.
